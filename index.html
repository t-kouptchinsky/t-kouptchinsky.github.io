Research

My reasearch revolves around the study of axioms derived from the game theory context. I focus on a reverse mathematical treatment of those. The three main notions I treat during my ongoing doctoral thesis are:

  1. Gales-Stewart determinacy in Cantor and Baire spaces;
  2. Turing determinacy and;
  3. Blackwell determinacy.
